<h4><strong>Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) for Use of ICT Resources</strong></h4>
<p>Technology supports my learning in school. I will use all devices, online
accounts and resources safely, respectfully and responsibly.</p>
<table style="minWidth: 75px">
<colgroup>
<col>
<col>
<col>
</colgroup>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>Be Responsible</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Use school devices and accounts only for learning.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Handle devices with care and report any damage.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Do not access or change settings or files without permission.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>Be Respectful</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Communicate politely and use kind words online.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Do not post or share rude, hurtful or offensive content.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Do not take photos or videos of others without permission.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>Be Safe</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Keep personal information and passwords private.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Avoid suspicious links or chatting with strangers online.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Report anything unsafe to a teacher or parent.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>Be Honest</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Use AI tools only if allowed and of the right age.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Do not submit AI work as your own.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Respect copyright. Always credit others’ work.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>Practise Balance</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Take regular breaks and rest your eyes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Limit screen time, especially before bedtime.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong><em>&nbsp;Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action in line with school rules.</em></strong>
