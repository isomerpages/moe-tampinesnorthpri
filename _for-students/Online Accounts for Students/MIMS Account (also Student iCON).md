<h4><strong>What is MIMS?</strong></h4>
<p>Your MIMS account lets you log in to many platforms, such as:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>SLS</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Google account</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Online forms and surveys (such as the termly check in surveys)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h4><strong>What is Student iCON?</strong></h4>
<p>Student iCON is your school digital account that gives you access to useful
learning tools. It includes apps like Google Classroom, Google Docs, Google
Slides, your school email, and Microsoft apps like Word, Excel and PowerPoint.</p>
<p>Together, these tools provide students with a safe and structured digital
environment for learning, communication, collaboration and productivity,
and are used to complement teaching and learning in school.</p>
<p></p>
<h4><strong>How do I log in?</strong></h4>
<p>When you see this page, it means you need to log in using your MIMS account.
You would have already learned how to sign in at school.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/MIMS.png">
</div>
<p></p>
<p><strong>Username: </strong>This is your student iCON email address.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Student’s email address will be based on (Full Name)@students.edu.sg</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Any space in the full name will be replaced with an underscore.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Should the student’s full name have words such as “Bte” and “s/o”, they
will not appear in the email address.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For cases where students share the same full name, we have added a numerical
number (e.g. Jon_Tan_1, Jon_Tan_2) to differentiate their email addresses.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Password: </strong>You have been given your password and have
learned how to log in with it at school.</p>
